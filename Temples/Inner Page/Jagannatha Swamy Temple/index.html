Elegantly dressed in chiseled architecture, Sri Jaganadha Swamy temple in visakhapatnam houses three deities, jagannath, Balaram and Shubadhra. Being one of the oldest Jagannath temples around, the olden day beauty of this temple is no comparison to the tourist that flocks here during the Ratha-Yatra festival.

Location

The Godes, who were of the Chatrasaal Gotra,were Shaktas, but after settling down in Andhra in the mid-17th century, they came under the influence of Vaishnavism over a period of time, partly because of their connection with Odisha. That is how the Jagannatha Swamy Temple of Visakhapatnam came into existence 183 years ago.


Location

The idols of Lord Jagannatha, his consort Subhadra and his brother Balbhadra were brought from Cuttack in Odisha. The orginal temple was built in a simple style, which was a confluence of the Kalinga and Dravidian styles of architecture.
